DISCLAMER

The service works in manual mode. Orders are not processed by a smart contract (for now). To place an order:
1. Fill in the required form items, click "Confirm".
2. Click "Make the Offer" and paste the order info from the exchange buffer into the dialog with the owner.
3. After (!!!) confirmation from the operator that the order has been accepted for processing, return to the order page and PAY for the order.

Order Summary

* You have created a domain rental order, it has been copied to the clipboard. Click "Make an offer" and paste it into the contact message to which you will be redirected.

Pay the Order

Step 1. Log in to the service via Ton Connect.

Step 2. Click the Pay button and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Warning!

* The service uses the standard ton connect protocol and the standard json manifest, in the connected connections of your wallet, the connection with the service is called Demo Dapp. The service does not have access to your wallet after connection and does not store data about it. This connection is necessary for automatic invoicing of payment, which will only occur with your consent by confirming the transaction.
